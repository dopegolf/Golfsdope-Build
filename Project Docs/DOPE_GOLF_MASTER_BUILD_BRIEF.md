## 0. THE WHY (read this first — it's the whole point)

Dope Golf isn't a merch idea. It's Ronnie's mission to build something that takes care of his wife Danielle and daughter Carolyn — permanently. Ronnie broke a brutal generational cycle to get here: lost his dad at 9, grew up around parental drug addiction, survived a car accident at 13 that killed his stepfather and permanently disabled his mother, went into foster care, lost both of his full brothers (Jake and Daniel) 11 months apart in 2019, and lost his home in a Northern California wildfire that also took half his town. Then he met Danielle, found faith, and rebuilt in Phoenix.

Everything about this brand — the name, "The Movement," the tone — is driven by: providing for Danielle and Carolyn, supporting his disabled mother, helping his sister, honoring Jake and Daniel's memory (and their two sons), and giving Carolyn a life with no financial ceiling on her dreams.

This context should quietly inform tone choices (authentic, resilient, "join the movement" energy is earned, not marketing fluff) — it does NOT belong in public-facing copy as a sob story. It's the engine, not the pitch.

## 1. LOCKED BRAND DECISIONS (do not deviate without Ronnie's sign-off)

- **Brand principle:** "Premium Foundation. Coliseum Energy." → the crest/logo carries credibility and trust; everything else (copy, color, energy) is bold and alive.
- **Logo:** Clean gold shield + interlocked "DG" monogram + "DOPE GOLF" wordmark below. **No crown.**
- **Taglines:**
  - Core: "Play Dope. Stay Dope."
  - Secondary: "Higher Vibes = Lower Scores."
  - Primary CTA: "Join The Movement"
- **Domain:** golfsdope.com is the live domain. **Never** show "DOPEGOLF.COM" in customer-facing copy.
- **Real socials (use these, nothing else):**
  - Instagram: @dopegolfcompany
  - Facebook: "Dope Golf Company"
  - YouTube: @DopegolfCompany — live at youtube.com/@dopegolfcompany, currently zero videos posted. This is meant to become a core traffic/community driver (content-first, "Breezy model") alongside the merch — not just a placeholder icon.
- **Roots:** Phoenix, Arizona. This should show up as a trust/identity signal (imagery, "From Phoenix" messaging), not buried.

## 2. VISUAL DIRECTION (Danni's direction, approved)

- **Palette:** Black + gold foundation, with an ember/copper Arizona-sunset accent color. Turquoise optional as a secondary pop.
- **Patterns:** Minimal — let the crest and color do the work, don't overdecorate.
- **Photography:** Lifestyle-first. Hero imagery should be real people celebrating at golden hour in a Sonoran desert setting — NOT product-on-white. The emotional hook is **belonging**, not apparel. Product-on-white is reserved for actual shop/product pages, not the homepage.
- **Mobile-first** design assumption throughout.
